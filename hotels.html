Hotels in Nepal

  • Hotel Yak & Yeti

    Located in the heart of Kathmandu, Hotel Yak & Yeti offers a blend of modern amenities and traditional Nepali architecture. The hotel features several restaurants, a spa, and a casino.

  • The Pavilions Himalayas

    The Pavilions Himalayas is a luxury eco-resort located in Pokhara. The resort offers stunning views of the Himalayan mountains and features an organic farm, a spa, and a swimming pool.

  • Tiger Tops Tharu Lodge

    Tiger Tops Tharu Lodge is a rustic retreat located in the Chitwan National Park. The lodge offers a chance to experience the wildlife and culture of Nepal up close, with activities like jungle safaris and cultural tours.
