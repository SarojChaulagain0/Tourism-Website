Featured Destinations in Nepal

Kathmandu

Kathmandu is the capital city of Nepal and a hub for tourism in the country. The city is home to many historic temples and palaces, as well as modern amenities like restaurants and shopping centers.

Pokhara

Pokhara is a picturesque city located near the Annapurna mountain range. It is a popular destination for trekkers and adventure enthusiasts, as well as those seeking a peaceful escape from the city.

Chitwan

Chitwan is a wildlife sanctuary located in the southern part of Nepal. It is home to a variety of animals, including tigers, rhinos, and elephants, as well as many species of birds and reptiles.
